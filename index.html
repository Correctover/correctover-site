What is the Reliability Layer?

Not a gateway. Not a router. Not infrastructure. It's the guarantee that your AI system works correctly.

Beyond Failover: Semantic Equivalence Verification

Traditional failover just retries when something breaks. Correctover verifies that the output is semantically equivalent to what was expected — using AST validation, behavioral equivalence checks, and 6-dimensional verification. When a failure is detected, MAPE-K control loop auto-recovers with 97.4% success rate.
