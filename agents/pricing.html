Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing, free tiers, and upgrades.

What are the hard limits on the free tier?
Each agent has a hard daily/monthly limit enforced client-side. Security Audit: 50 scans/day. Compliance Check: unlimited checks (no certificate). Runtime Guard: 50 calls/day (1,500/month). Once the limit is hit, the agent stops executing until the next day/month. No credit card required to start.
Do I need to pay to use the agents?
No. All three agents are free to install and use within the daily/monthly limits. You only pay when you need unlimited usage, CI/CD integration, official certification, or enterprise features. Install with pip install correctover-{name}.
How does the Security Audit Pro plan work?
Pro is $499/month and includes unlimited scans, SARIF output for CI/CD, GitHub PR integration, Slack notifications, and custom detection rules. Upgrade instantly via the "Upgrade to Pro" link on the Security Audit page.
What does the Compliance Certification include?
Certification costs $2,999 one-time plus $999/year renewal. It includes an official CCS certificate, a "Correctover Certified" badge for your README, public registry listing, automated re-certification scheduling, and a compliance audit report (PDF). The free compliance check runs all 18 checks but does not issue a certificate.
What's included in the Runtime Guard Enterprise plan?
Enterprise is $1,999/month and includes unlimited calls, custom guardrail policies, multi-agent deployment, dedicated support with SLA, and 1-year audit log retention. See the Runtime Guard page for details.
Can I use all three agents together?
Absolutely. The agents are designed to work together: Security Audit finds vulnerabilities in your code, Compliance Check certifies your MCP server meets standards, and Runtime Guard protects your agents in production. Each has its own free tier and pricing.
How do I upgrade from free to paid?
Click the "Upgrade" or "Get Started" button on any agent page. You'll be directed to our payment portal (Stripe/Paddle). Once payment is processed, your limits are instantly removed and your account is upgraded. No re-installation required — just update your license key.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. All paid plans come with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us at wangguigui@correctover.com for a full refund.
What about enterprise / on-premise deployment?
All agents support on-premise deployment for Enterprise customers. This includes air-gapped installation, custom fault taxonomy, SIEM integration, SAML SSO, and dedicated support. Use the contact form below to get in touch.
