FAQ

Do you offer a free trial or POC?

Yes. We offer a 7-day free trial (no credit card) and a 15-30 day paid POC for Professional and Enterprise plans. During the POC we deploy in your environment and provide a full CCS-style audit report at the end.

Can we do outcome-based pricing?

Yes. For Enterprise plans we offer outcome-based pricing where fees are tied to verified interception metrics. This is negotiated per engagement.

How does deployment work?

30 seconds via npm, PyPI, or MCP protocol. Works as a sidecar, SDK, or standalone server. No infrastructure changes required. Supports Cloudflare Workers, Docker, Render.com, and bare metal.

What AI frameworks do you support?

All major frameworks. Our 24 CCS rules have been validated against CrewAI, AutoGen, LiteLLM, LangGraph, Haystack, AG2, LlamaIndex, and Docker MCP. The runtime hooks work at the MCP/tool-call layer, so any framework using MCP tools is supported.

How do you handle false positives?

Our validated false positive rate is <0.3%, confirmed across 1,730+ findings. Custom rules include a tuning period to minimize noise for your specific environment.

Can we pay via invoice / PO?

Yes. Enterprise plans support purchase orders, wire transfers, and standard net-30 invoicing. We work with US, EU, and APAC procurement teams.
